Pattern Snap!

Spot matching patterns before your opponent. First to call SNAP wins the round!

How to Play

  1. Click Deal — both players get a word card
  2. Look for words with the same pattern (e.g., both V-e, both FLOSS)
  3. First to click their SNAP button wins — but only if it's actually a match!
  4. Wrong snap = opponent gets the point
